Hello, you can adjust the movement speed of your perspective (camera view) within the AiHouse design interface.

Here are the detailed operation steps:

1.  Locate the **Camera Settings** in the lower right corner of the design interface.
2.  Within the Camera Settings, find the option for **Moving Speed**.
3.  Adjust the slider or input a value to set your desired movement speed.
