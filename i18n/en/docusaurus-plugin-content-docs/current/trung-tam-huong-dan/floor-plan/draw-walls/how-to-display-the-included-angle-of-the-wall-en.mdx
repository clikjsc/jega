---
title: How to display the included angle of the wall
description: Learn how to display the angle between two walls in AiHouse using the Wall Settings.
icon: /images/icons/floor-plan.svg
---

Hello, please click on the tools on the right toolbar of the design page - Settings - Wall Settings - Check the Show Angle, and then click on the corresponding wall at the angle to display. (Note: If the two walls are perpendicular and exactly 90 degrees, the degree will not be displayed.)

![Wall Settings - Show Angle](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/5f66e7d4-b962-4a12-bb29-71a728739764.png)
