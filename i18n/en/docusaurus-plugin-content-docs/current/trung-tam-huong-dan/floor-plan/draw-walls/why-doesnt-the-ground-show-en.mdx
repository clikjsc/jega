---
title: Why doesn't the ground show?
description: Troubleshoot and resolve issues where the ground or floor is not displaying correctly in AiHouse, including checks for wall types and transparency settings.
icon: /images/icons/floor-plan.svg
---

1.  After the wall is drawn, if it is a section or a wall of different heights, please replace it with a pillar.

    ![Replace Wall with Pillar](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/fc53b8f2-cee3-4612-927a-9a9801c8c8a8.png)

2.  Check the wall transparency. It is recommended to set the "Selected effect transparency" to 100%;

    ![Check Wall Transparency Settings](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/d763d05e-1c8b-4cc6-bcdb-ce3d6a3306a1.png)
