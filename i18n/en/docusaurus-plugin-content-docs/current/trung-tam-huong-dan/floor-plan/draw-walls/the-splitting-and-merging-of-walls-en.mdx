Hello, by using the functions of wall splitting and merging, it achieves the effect of quickly modifying the layout of the house type.

![Wall Splitting and Merging Overview](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/e263f550-8ba0-461f-bc0a-02bfa2e01f37.png)

If it is a merging operation of two house types (split doors and windows will move along, designs within the space will not move along. It is recommended to complete the setting of house type splitting and merging before conducting designs of accessories, etc.) The operation steps are as follows:

1.  Click and select the house type with the left mouse button. The selected one will appear blue, and select Move;

    ![Select House Type and Move](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/5e93201c-4136-4131-8ebf-d2fc772708c3.png)

2.  Press and hold the left mouse button and move it to the position to be merged, and confirm.

    ![Move to Merge Position](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/4eb61ee0-ed31-46bc-b997-0f5a56b103ff.png)
