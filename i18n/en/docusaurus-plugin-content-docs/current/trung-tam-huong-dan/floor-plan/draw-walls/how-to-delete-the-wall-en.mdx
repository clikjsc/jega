---
title: How to delete the wall
description: Learn how to delete a single section of a wall or all walls in a plan in AiHouse.
icon: /images/icons/floor-plan.svg
---

Hello, if the wall is drawn incorrectly, for a single section, right-click and directly press the DELETE key to delete it, or press ctrl+Z to undo and re-click to draw the wall. If all the walls of the current plan need to be deleted, you can click "New" to create a new house type.
