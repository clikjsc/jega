Hello, please draw with free modeling. The operation steps are as follows.

1.  Click to enter the free modeling interface;

    ![Enter Free Modeling Interface](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/931f2240-8283-4a9b-bc13-951b25a415fc.png)

2.  Click with the mouse to draw lines - select the line segments to draw the shape of the inclined wall and then stretch the shape;

    ![Draw Line Segments for Inclined Wall](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/36bb5021-8c94-44de-90c5-8828b4e2032d.png)

3.  Completion effect.

    ![Completed Inclined Wall](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/d3dcd90d-28df-4242-86b8-5394ea8259ff.png)
