## FAQ

### Q: Who can create a Circle?

A: Organizations (enterprises or stores) approved for a Design Circle can assign creation permissions to specific users, though only admins have this right by default.

### Q: How many Circles can one organization create?

A: Each organization can create up to 1 Circle. Once the Circle has 10 members or 100 shared assets, the organization becomes eligible to create one more Circle. For additional capacity, please contact our customer support.

### Q:Who can join a Circle?

A:

-   Public Circles: Any organization's administrator can apply to join. After joining, all members within the organization are added by default.
-   Platform Circles: Individual users can join.
-   Private Circles: Joining is by invitation only.

### Q:How many Circles can one organization join?

A: Up to 10 Circles by default. For more, please contact Circle customer support.
