---
title: How to draw a low wall (1721)
---

There are two ways to draw a low wall:

### Method 1: Use the "Low Wall" tool under "Draw Wall"

1.  Click **Draw Wall** > **Low Wall**.
    ![Draw low wall step 1](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/143a2a3a-9e9a-49f3-b0b6-57010089390d.png)
2.  Enter the desired wall height.
    ![Draw low wall step 2](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/01916a39-b697-4e6a-80a3-ebde08883103.png)
3.  Draw the low wall in the viewport.
    ![Draw low wall step 3](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/15a638a4-459d-47c0-8582-8a7227c80069.png)

### Method 2: Adjust an existing wall to a low wall

1.  Select the wall you want to adjust.
2.  In the right-hand properties bar, select **Low Wall**.
    ![Adjust wall to low wall step 1](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/b8d80a8e-9739-4f4a-9381-957821270919.png)
3.  Enter the desired wall height.
    ![Adjust wall to low wall step 2](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/0a9a5a7a-8b8a-4a3a-9a8a-7a8a7a8a7a8a.png)

    Result:
    ![Low wall result](https://ai-study1.3vjia.com/aihouse-web/7a8b8c8d-9e9f-0a0b-1c1d-2e2f3a3b3c3d.png)
