---
title: How to quickly modify the materials of the wall or the floor
description: Learn how to quickly change wall or floor materials in AiHouse using the Public Library and drag-and-drop functionality in 3D mode.
icon: /images/icons/floor-plan.svg
---

Hello, left-click the Public library - Finishing - Ceramic tile to filter the material. In the 3D mode, left-click and drag the texture to the floor/wall. (If you want to quickly lay the four walls, you can hold down the Ctrl key and drag.)
