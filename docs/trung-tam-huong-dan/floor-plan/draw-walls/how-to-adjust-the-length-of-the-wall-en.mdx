---
title: How to adjust the length of the wall
description: Learn how to view and modify the length of walls in AiHouse's 2D mode by clicking on the wall and editing the displayed distance values.
icon: /images/icons/floor-plan.svg
---

You can click on the wall in the 2D state to view the distance between the walls, and click on the distance to modify the value for movement. (Note: Only closed walls can have their dimensions modified)
